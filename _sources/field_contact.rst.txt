.. _d:contact:

Contact (O)
-----------
A reference to contact information for this resource (occurrences: 0-n).

**Allowed values, examples, other constraints**

List of names or institutions. Could also be e-mail adress, url of contact form etc.

Example
~~~~~~~
.. code-block:: xml
   :linenos:

   <contacts>
   <contact>
   example@community.org
   </contact>
   <contact>
   https://community.org/contact
   </contact>
  	<contact>
  	 Research Data Office (University of Groningen)
  	</contact>
   </contacts>
