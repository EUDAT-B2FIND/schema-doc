.. _d:relatedidentifier:

Related Identifier (O)
----------------------
Identifiers of related resources (occurrence: 0-n).

**Allowed values, examples, other constraints**

Should be a resolvable URI.

Example
~~~~~~~
.. code-block:: xml
   :linenos:

    <relatedIdentifier>
     https://doi.org/10.1029/96JB03945
    </relatedIdentifier>
