Spatial Coverage (O)¶

Spatial region or named place where the data was gathered or which the data is about (occurrence: 0-n). If geocoordinates are provided, the region will be displayed on the map. B2FIND offers the following possibilities to display geographical information: geoLocationPlace geoLocationPoint geoLocationBox geoLocationPolygon

Allowed values, examples, other constraints Recommended, in accordance with DataCite: Use WGS 84 (World Geodetic System) coordinates. Use only decimal numbers for coordinates. Longitudes are -180 to 180(0 is Greenwich, negative numbers are west, positive numbers are east), Latitudes are -90 to 90 (0 is the equator; negative numbers are south, positive numbers north).

Example¶

1<keywords>
2     <keyword>Chemistry</subject>
3     <keyword>Catalysis</subject>
4     <keyword>Confinement</subject>
5     <keyword>Metathesis</subject>
6</keywords>
