Contact (O)

A reference to contact information for this resource (occurrences: 0-n).

Allowed values, examples, other constraints

List of names or institutions. Could also be e-mail address, URL of contact form etc.

Example

1<contacts>
2   <contact>example@community.org</contact>
3   <contact>https://community.org/contact</contact>
4   <contact>Research Data Office (University of Groningen)</contact>
5</contacts>
